Your Thoughts Stay Yours

We built Rise & Reflect with privacy at its core. Because the most personal conversations deserve the highest level of protection.

Zero Server Storage

All your journal entries, voice recordings, and insights live only on your device. We never store, access, or upload your personal data to our servers.

Anonymous AI Processing

When AI helps with reflection, only anonymized prompts are sent—never your identity, history, or personal details. Each conversation is private and isolated.

No Tracking or Selling

We don't track your journal content, analyze your emotions for advertising, or sell your data. Your reflections are yours alone.

Device-First Architecture: Your iPhone is the vault. All processing, storage, and insights generation happen locally. If you delete the app, everything is gone—because it was never anywhere else.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rise & Reflect?

Rise & Reflect (also known as Rise and Reflect) is a daily reflection phone call app. Not recording - actual real-time conversations with AI. Talk naturally while driving, walking, or anywhere. It features morning and evening phone sessions, with all data stored privately on your device. Like therapy, but daily.

Is Rise and Reflect private?

Yes, Rise & Reflect is completely private. All journal entries, voice recordings, and insights are stored only on your iPhone. We never upload, store, or access your personal data on our servers. Your thoughts stay yours—100% on-device storage with zero data collection.

How long are Rise & Reflect sessions?

Each Rise and Reflect phone call takes just 5 minutes. You can do one morning conversation and one evening conversation per day. Quick live phone conversations that fit into your actual life while driving, walking, or anywhere—no elaborate rituals required.

How does Rise and Reflect track my goals?

Rise & Reflect uses AI to identify goals and patterns from your real-time phone conversations. It remembers your strategies, what works for you, and helps you build on past successes over time. During live calls, the AI asks thoughtful questions that help you discover your own solutions, then recalls them when you need them most.

Is Rise & Reflect therapy or a therapist replacement?

No, Rise and Reflect is not therapy or a therapist replacement. It's a personal reflection companion—a guide that helps you guide yourself. We focus on helping you process your thoughts, recognize patterns, and discover your own strategies for growth. For mental health concerns, always consult a licensed professional.
